OUR MISSION.

Give our partners the tools to 100x.

Altum Labs’ digital platform is a frictionless one-stop-shop for compliance, communication, and cost-reduction. Perfecting the newest technologies available, our platform enables each of our users to save time, money, and effort.
Think of us as the FDA for Everything Else.

Producers (Cultivators/Processors) can reduce overhead, put products to market faster, and increase lead conversions via our platform: They can compare/shop for cost-effective testing, receive test results faster, see regulators approve their products for sale in real-time, and show their customers our QR-based “stamp of approval” that intuitively conveys important information via OCM-compliant labels. We allow Labs to save time and money by automatically processing their GCMS, LCMS, HPLC, and UHPLC data and sending results directly to regulators and producers. Regulators save time and effort by accessing and approving/rejecting samples that pass/fail their standards all in one place. Consumers can easily access safety and composition information about the products that go through our system via our QR-based verification system.
